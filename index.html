About

I think it is better to sacrifice some happiness of the present so that I can get hand of a bigger treasure in the future. With an outgoing character and dedication towards any skill I learned, I have great experiences in all the works I have done outside academics.

A few things I have done so far

  • Singing for the Radio
  • Singing at home
  • Volunteering
  • Having the role of Secretariat Board in Intraschool MUN
  • Participation at the Science Fair
  • Cleaning the streets
  • Planting new plants

Experiences

Singing

Both singing, listening and dancing to music has played a role in lessening and healing my stress. Learning to play the harmonium and learning to sing melifluously to the beat and switching tunes smoothly in synchronization with the harmonium has been a beautiful voyage for my voice. I even used to sing at home infront of my relatives and family. I got my first earning after singing for the National Radio "Bangladesh Betar".

Volunteering

I volunteered for a lot of school events. Like, I was the USG- Under Secretary General- of Hospitality in Intraschool MUN. It was a tough work to go and buy food and drinks for the delegates and then distribute it to them, also managing the event and monitoring the delegates. But overall, it was a memorable experience.

Participation in Science Fair

My participation in Science Fairs in my school enriched my scientific knowledge. I also learned how to do projects alone, which strengthened my self-reliance.

Cleaning the streets

I was always determined to work for the improvement of the surroundings, and seeing the streets clean when you walk on them is an addition to mental peace. Streets without dirt also help prevent visual pollution and the spread of diseases.

Planting new plants

This helped me contribute to the greenery of our green country. I was always dedicated to work towards ecological improvements and the aesthetics of nature.
